Hotlines and Reporting Sites

If you are experiencing any immediate danger, please call 911

Rape, Abuse & Incest National Network (RAINN)

RAINN is a non-profit organization that stands against sexual violence. You can call 1-800-656-HOPE (1-800-656-4673).

Self Helpline

Safe Helpline is the hotline created by the Department of Defense (DoD). Safe Helpline helps you learn about different resources and different ways that you can heal. It also has pages that explain the effects of sexual assult and explains what a sexual assult forensic exam (SAFE) is. They also have a mobile app that you can use. You can call 1-877-995-5247 to access their helpline.

StrongHearts Native Helpline

StrongHearts Native Helpline is accessible all day long. They offer information about legal referrals and help victims and survivors of sexual assault find support. They also offer advice for relationships. To access their helpline, call 1-844-762-8483.

Victim Connect

Victim Connect allows you to learn about what sexual assault is, and how to go about the situation. You can learn abou the various options that you have, and better understand your rights. You can call 1-855-484-2846 to access the hotline.

Pennsylvania Coalition Against Rape

The Pennsylvania Coalition Against Rape offers a toll free hotline for victims of sexual assault. They also allow the media to call them to learn more about topics related to sexual assault. You can call them at 1-888-772-7227.

Women Organized Against Rape aka WOAR

Women Organized Against Rape (WOAR) is a non-profit organization based in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. They want to be able to use treatment services, educational programs, and advocating for the rights of victims of sexual assault to get rid of all forms of sexual adviolence. Their free services include court accompaniment, forensic exam accompaniment, therapy, access to a 24/7 hotline, etc. You can access their hotline by calling or texting 1-215-985-3333.

Online Chats

After Silence

After Silence is an online support group that wants to empower survivors of sexual assult and rape. They want to allow them to come together through communication on their forum. The forum has rules and guidelines, and it has a message board and chat room. You are also able to learn about other survivors' stories.

DailyStrength

Daily Strength is made up of multiple support and community groups. The topics covered range from mental health to skin and hair. You can find the many different support groups here. All of the groups have rules. Support groups were created by DailyStrength themselves, and community groups were created by DailyStrength membres. This is the support group and page specifically for rape. You will be able to receive support and advice from others, and learn about other people's stories. They also have a support group and page for sexual abuse. It talks about what sexual abuse is.

Pandora's Project

Pandora's Project is a non-profit organization that focuses on supporting and providing resources to victims, and their families and friends, of rape and sexual abuse. They have an inclusive online forum called Pandora's Aquarium, which has multiple rules and guidelines. Pandora's Aquarium is meant for "peer support". But if you go back to their homepage, linked above, you are able to able to access various resources and articles for more information. Their website explains what rape, sexual assult, and sexual abuse is. The staff from Pandora's Project are volunteers that also happen to be sexual violence survivors

Rape, Abuse & Incest National Network (RAINN)

RAINN is a non-profit organization that stands against sexual violence. You are able to talk to a real staff member in real time. This will allow you to talk about your experiences and options to move forwards, as well as receive emotional support. You are also able to talk to their Iris Chatbot, which helps you find things on their website. In addition, it helps you find various resources online. The Iris Chatbot is not meant for emotional support. You can also access their chats from their mobile app.

Safe Helpline

Safe Helpline is the hotline created by the Department of Defense (DoD). They have two chats. One chat is a general chat that allows you talk to a staff member who will support you. The other chat, Safe HelpRoom, is a group chat for survivors of sexual assult in the military. Both chats keep your information and whatever you say confidential. Safe Helpline helps you learn about different resources and different ways that you can heal. It also has pages that explain the effects of sexual assult and explains what a sexual assult forensic exam (SAFE) is. They also have a mobile app that you can use.

Safe Horizon

Safe Horizon is a non-profit organization based in New York City that advocates for many social issues such as child abuse, human trafficking, rape and sexual assult, etc. All of the issues that they talk about can be found on their home page, which is the link above. On their page for rape and sexual assult, they talk about what rape and sexual assult are. They have a SafeChat that will allow you to talk to an advocate to receive information about your options, and they will answer your questions. They also talk about consent and conercion. There are also programs listed that can help you recover.
