Meet The Team!

“A woman is in full circle. Within her is the power to create, nurture and transform.”

Diane Mariechild

On average 1 out of 5 women encounter uncomfortable,non-consensual experiences, and most of these cases do not get reported at all. These women tend to feel unsafe when undergoing these experiences so our team has decided to create a web application that works in these women’s favors. This is an all inclusive women designed web application that will push for the idea of women of all color, race, and age to feel safe.
