Articles

“23 Tips for a Healthy Pregnancy” by Laurie Herr

There are a lot of changes in lifestyle that comes with being pregnant. This article talks about the change of diet that one should have, how to stay healthy while being pregnant, and what exercise is allowed during pregnancy. The article also reminds pregnant women to check their medications.

“Alternative Therapies for Pregnancy Discomfort and Pain”

While pregnancy can be exciting, it also brings new, uncomfortable sensations. This article discusses what pregnant women should do if they are experiencing discomfort and pain.

“Infographic: Your pregnancy health care journey”

There is a lot of paperwork and doctors visits that need to be taken care of while you are pregnant. This article talks about what you need to know in order to fill out the paper work, why you should be visiting your Ob/Gyn, what happens during prenatal visits, and what procedures you may need to undergo.

"Pregnancy week by week”

Each trimester brings changes to both the baby and those who are pregnant. This article talks about what happens for both the pregnant woman and the baby during each trimester. Besides the previous statement, this article also talks about possible issues one may encounter during pregnancy.


Books

Mayo Clinic Guide to a Healthy Pregnancy, Second Edition by Myra J. Wick

 You can buy the book off of the Mayo Clinic's website.

This is a reference manual made by the Mayo Clinic. It allows people to understand the basics in terms of prenatal care. It also talks about childbirth and the different trends that have come with it. Lastly, recommendations and the various technologies used throughout the process are discussed.

What to Expect When You're Expecting by Heidi Murkoff

 You can buy the paperback version or the hardcover version of the book on Amazon.

Murkoff discusses many of the important topics a pregnant woman must be knowledgable about during her pregnancy. Issues such as sore feet and hurting back are discussed. She also gives practical and realistic advice. The recommended medications are discusses, as well as the needed change in diet. Bathroom rituals such as skincare are mentioned as well. Most importantly, the different options for giving birth are discussed.


Magazines

Parents

You can subscribe to the Parents magazine through this link.

This magazine talks about parenthood, and pregnancy throughout the school years. Not only does it give insight to the health, behaviors, safety, and nutrition of children, but it gives tips for taking care of them.


Websites

Pampers

Pampers is a well known brand for expecting parents. Their website has various articles about how the baby bump develops, and the different pregnancy struggles and treatments. They also talk about how one should be going about each trimester. There is also information about what happens if you are pregnant with twins or triplets. They also discuss different exercises that are safe to do while pregnant.

Parents

Just like the magazine, the Parents website talks about your parenthood. It guides you through the school years, and how your kids should be behaving. Not only does it give tips for taking care of your children, but it gives nutrition and safety information as well.

What to Expect

Heidi Murkoff, author of What to Expect When You're Expecting founded this website. There are various links for you to learn about what should be happening during each week of pregnancy, and what should be happening when the baby is a newborn and a toddler. There is a section for the baby products that you and the baby will need. Besides all of that, there is information about all the appointments that need to be scheduled during the journey, and why they need to be made.
